          <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Within the economic classes, individuals are chosen based on their capacity to achieve economic stability in Canada.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><a href="

          <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Canada invites accomplished business professionals who are in search of new opportunities and challenges. The Business Immigration Program is crafted to promote and simplify the entry of these individuals.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><a href="
https://sgrigorov.github.io/website/permanent-residence/businness-class"><strong>Learn more</strong></a></p>
          <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">The primary aim of the Family Class program is  to ensure the reunion of families in Canada. </p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><a href="
https://sgrigorov.github.io/website/permanent-residence/family-class"><strong>Learn more</strong></a></p>
          <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Canada, as a signatory to the 1951 Convention relating to the Status of Refugees, acknowledges and respects the right of individuals to make a claim for refugee protection, whether they are in Canada or outside its borders.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><a href="
https://sgrigorov.github.io/website/permanent-residence/refugee-class"><strong>Learn more</strong></a></p>
          <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">H&amp;C grants the individual permanent residency or exempt them from certain rules, if the Minister believes there are compassionate reasons to do so.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><a href="
https://sgrigorov.github.io/website/permanent-residence/humanitarianandcompassionategrounds"><strong>Learn more</strong></a></p>
          <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">A Temporary Resident Visa (TRV) is an official document provided by a visa office and inserted into a person's passport. It serves as proof that the individual has fulfilled the necessary conditions for temporary entry into Canada.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><a href="
https://sgrigorov.github.io/website/temporary-residence1/visit-canada"><strong>Learn more</strong></a></p>
